How it works
Stage 1
We discuss your needs and intentions; we agree with our policy. You share the job spec. We start the search. We do a preliminary screening: check the compliance with the required technical and soft skills, check English level, do a general background check, we screen for the desired salary level and its accordance to a specific local market; skip unsuitable applicants. You get a group of the candidates interested exactly in your job opportunity and ready for further steps.
Stage 2
You choose the candidates for the interview. We help with the scheduling of the interviews. We control the process and support you with all the required stuff such as providing candidates with all your technical tasks, collecting additional data such as portfolios, work examples, etc. We work with frequently asked questions as well as with complaints and motivation. We provide you with new resumes for your review in case it's needed. As a result, you meet your right candidates.
Stage 3
We make official offers to chosen candidates. We are responsible for all the legal matters, such as documents collecting, signing agreements, etc. When document packages are ready, you start your direct work with your contractors. Each month our platform collect contractors' monthly hours and you receive an invoice which is paid 'On receipt'. You always work and build your own relationship with the employees. Meanwhile, we make every effort to keep this collaboration as the most efficient one.
